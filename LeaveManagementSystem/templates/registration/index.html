Welcome to the Employee Leave Management System. This system streamlines the leave application and approval process, providing a seamless experience for both employees and administrators.

If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to explore the available features or contact our support team.

Our system allows employees to submit leave requests conveniently through the online portal. Once submitted, the requests are promptly reviewed by supervisors or administrators, ensuring a quick and efficient approval process. You can track the status of your leave applications and view your leave history for better planning.

For administrators, the system offers comprehensive reporting features through the Report Generator. Generate insightful reports on leave trends, department-wise statistics, and more. Stay informed and make data-driven decisions to optimize workforce management.

The Requests Received section provides a centralized view of pending leave requests, allowing supervisors and administrators to manage approvals and denials effectively. This feature helps in maintaining a balanced workflow within the organization.

We understand the importance of timely communication. Our system sends automated notifications at each stage of the leave process, keeping everyone informed. Whether your leave request is approved or requires additional information, you'll receive timely updates.

Explore the user-friendly interface to navigate through the various sections effortlessly. The Home page provides quick access to essential features, and the navigation bar simplifies movement across different modules.
